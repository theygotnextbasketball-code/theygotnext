Luke Williams

Guard · 6’0” · Olathe, KS
Available
Agency: JCK Sports Group

About

Luke Williams is a senior guard out of Olathe, Kansas, who completed his collegiate career at Charleston Southern University (D1 — Big South Conference) in 2025–26. Playing at the Division I level, Williams established himself as a reliable two-way guard capable of contributing on both ends of the floor.

In 20 games with 18 starts, Williams averaged 10.6 PPG, 3.0 RPG and 4.4 APG while shooting 45.0% from the field and 39.6% from three. His assist-to-turnover ratio of 3.7 highlights his efficiency and composure as a ball handler, while his three-point shooting percentage makes him a credible floor spacer at the next level.

At 6’0” and 180 lbs, Williams brings D1 pedigree, shooting efficiency and playmaking ability to the table. His 39.6% from three over 101 attempts is a legitimate selling point for professional scouts — that volume and accuracy from deep is exactly what European clubs look for in an import guard.

Williams is a 2026 graduate and is actively seeking professional opportunities overseas.
