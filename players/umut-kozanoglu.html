Umut Kozanoglu

Point Guard · 185 cm · BBC Rendsburg Twisters · Regionalliga (Germany)

About

Umut Kozanoglu has been one of the most productive guards in Germany’s Regionalliga during the 2025–26 season. Through the first half of the campaign with BBC Rendsburg Twisters, the Turkish point guard has consistently controlled the tempo, scored at a high level, and delivered from beyond the arc.

His ability to create shots off the dribble, punish defenses from deep, and read the floor makes him a constant offensive threat and one of the top performers in Regionalliga North.
