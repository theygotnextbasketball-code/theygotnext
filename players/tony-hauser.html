Tony Hauser

Power Forward · 6’8” · Limerick Celtics · Irish Super League · Ireland

About

Tony Hauser is a 6’8” power forward from Charlotte, NC. A Fayetteville State graduate in his second professional season, Hauser established himself as one of the more productive bigs in the Irish Super League with the Limerick Celtics.

What stands out beyond the numbers is his skill level in the post — soft touch around the basket, good footwork, and the ability to create his own shot in the paint that you don’t always see from a player this early in their pro career.
